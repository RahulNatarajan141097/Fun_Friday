Instructions for the Game

  • We will split the team based on the available members.

  • Which team will go first and so on will be based on the lucky draw.

  • There will be 4 different categories and 5 different levels of questions, everytime you can choose one from it in your turn.

  • For the 100 and 200 points you will have 1 minute to answer, you need to answer before the timer turns to 0.

  • For the 300 point you will have 2 minutes to answer, you need to answer before the timer turns to 0.

  • For the 400 and 500 points you will have 3 minutes to answer, you need to answer before the timer turns to 0.

  • For the 400 and 500 points questions there will be a clue you can use but if the clue is used 20% of the ponit will be deducted for particular question.

  • If you answer the question correctly you will get the points based on the points of question.

  • Eg. If you answered 200 point question correctly you will get 200 ponits.

  • Likewise if you answer the question wrongly you will loose 20% of the points from your total points.

  • Eg. If you answered 200 point question wrongly you will loose 20 ponits from your total points.

  • Points will be calculated using the ponits calculator.

  • The points are based on the difficulty level of the question

  • Should not use mobiles or PC's or Laptop during game time.

  • If there is any of the following activity found the points will not be added to your team.

  • You can choose your question by saying category name and points

  • There could be other answers to the question but the one displayed in the screen is will be considered as final

  • We need to spend the time effectively without arguments for answers
